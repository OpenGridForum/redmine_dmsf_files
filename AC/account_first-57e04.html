Welcome to GridForgeTM!

You are now a registered user on GridForge, your Collaborative Software Development platform.

To participate in a group project, please email your login name to the chairperson of the project you wish to join. Once the chair or representative of the project adds your name to the project membership, you have will have access to project artifacts and be able to participate in the activities of the group.

You do not have to become a member of a group project to access its documents or participate in its discussion forums. However, if you are a "core" participant of the group meetings, it would be best to request membership from the group chair.

Some introductory information is available on the GGF GridForge Request Center. You may also wish to read through the GridForge Documentation so that you can take full advantage of GridForge.
