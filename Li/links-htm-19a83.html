As part of scoping the group, we have also been trying to work out which groups we are going to have a relationship with. The idea is that we nominate someone who is active in both GRAAP and the related group who can act as a link. We already have people nominated to be our links to the following groups:
  • Open Grid Services Architecture (OGSA-WG)
  • Grid Protocol Architecture (GPA-RG)
  • Grid Security Infrastructure (GSI-WG)
  • CIM based Grid Schema (CGS-WG)
  • Scheduling Dictionary (SD-WG)
We are also looking for contacts in the following groups:
  • Distributed Resource Management Application API (DRMAA-WG)
  • New Productivity Initiative (NPI-WG)
To volunteer to be our link to one of these groups, you can send us an e-mail. If there are other groups you feel we should be linking to, then you can post something on the mailing list.
