Minutes of the 4-Feb-2003 CGS-WG telephone conference

February 4, 2003

A CGS-WG telecon was held on Februrary 4, 2003 (8AM Pacific time). Attendees were:

Viktor Mihajlovski (IBM Germany)
Andrea Westerinen (DMTF and Cisco)
Peter Gietz (DAASI)
Larry Flon (USC/ISI) (only a short while)

Agenda

  1. Agenda bashing
  2. Demo plans for GGF7
  3. Time slot plans for GGF7
  4. Work relationship to JSDL group
  5. Any other business
  1. Agenda bashing
    No changes to the agenda were made
     
  2. Demo plans for GGF7
    • Viktor agreed to set up (with Tom to deliver) a CIM event processing demo.
    • Andrea agreed to set up and deliver a CIM Browser demo
    • Peter said that Andrea's demo would be the most important of the two, given our focus on JSIM. The event demo would be showing capabilities of CIM that might factor into the future for JSIM.

  3. Time slot plans for GGF7 
    • Peter will request one 90 minute slot, to be allocated roughly as:
      • 5 mins Agenda
      • 15 mins Presentation of current JSIM model and open issues
      • 30 mins Discussion of open issues
      • 20 mins Demo of CIM Browser and JSIM classes (static instances)
      • 20 mins Demo of event processing with CIM
         
  4. Work relationship to Job Submission Description Language group
    • Peter proposed working more closely with the JSDL-WG.  See http://www.epcc.ed.ac.uk/~ali/WORK/GGF/JSDL-WG/. Where semantics of job attributes are overlapping, they should be the same between the groups, and we should maintain open communication.
       
  5. Any other business
    Andrea made some comments on the current document about the weak binding of jobs to queues. She will send an email to the list with her comments on the current JSIM draft.
