Minutes of the 27-May-2003 CGS-WG telephone conference

A CGS-WG telecon was held on May 27, 2003 (8AM Pacific time). Attendees were:

Larry Flon (USC/ISI)
Tom Roney (NCSA)
Viktor Mihajlovski (IBM Germany)
Ellen Stokes (IBM)

Agenda

  1. Review Open Issues, and close as many as possible. See http://bugzilla.gridforum.org/cgs-wg for current issues.
  2. Any new business?
