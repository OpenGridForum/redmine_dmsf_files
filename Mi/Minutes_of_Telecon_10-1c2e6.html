Minutes of the 8-Apr-2003 CGS-WG telephone conference

Tom Roney (NCSA)

  3. Any new business (GGF8?)

  2. Discuss issue of rendering JSIM schema in LDAP and/or XML

We discussed delivering JSIM, in addition to UML and MOF, as LDAP and/or XML.  It was suggested and agreed to that there is not much value in working on an LDAP schema at this point in time.  At least it is more vital that we produce an XSD that is compatible with the OGSA work.  There are no tools yet to convert MOF to XSD, so this will need to be a mostly manual process.  But we don't see it as very difficult, just time consuming.  Peter agreed to do the initial work on the XSD, and Ellen will send some CRM samples of MOF to XSD conversion that will be available here.

  1. Any new business

It was proposed and agreed to that Larry should be made a co-chair of the group.
