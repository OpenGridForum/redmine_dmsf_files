The CMM-WG will have one session in GGF10,
in March 12, 8:30 a.m. - 10 a.m., room 2014B

The main objectives of the sessions are:
(1) Introduce the contents of the OASIS WSDM specs and explain their plans
For more information on WSDM, their web page is at:
http://www.oasis-open.org/committees/tc_home.php?wg_abbrev=wsdm
(2) Explain and discuss the contents of the Gap Analysis document
The document will be posted in the week before GGF10 at:
https://forge.gridforum.org/docman2/ViewCategory.php?group_id=107&category_id=622

The agenda (still preliminary) is:

(a) Early discussion (attendance list, note taker assignment, etc.) - 5 minutes
(b) Background on current activities (collaboration with OASIS WSDM, gap
analysis) and updates for people not attending teleconferences  - 5 minutes
(c) Introduction to the WSDM specs and plans - 30 minutes
(d) Review and discussion of the gap analysis document - 45 minutes
(e) Future plans - 5 minutes
