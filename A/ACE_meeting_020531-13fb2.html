Minutes from ACE-grid meeting on the AccessGrid

May 31, 2002 
     
Here are brief notes from the meeting and draft agenda for GGF5:

* We are noticing a lot of cross over between individual scenario
  drafts. Recommend creating a final document with a merged set of
  requirements plus appended individual scenarios to see nuances
  particular to the application area.

* Try and get individual scenario drafts out over the weekend.

* Try and get a merged document by Mid June
